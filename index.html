CDHAI Director Dr. Gordon Gao Highlights AI and MSIS Program at Orientation Event

August 2024 - Johns Hopkins Bloomberg Center

September 11, 2024
Gordon Gao Images

This week, the MSIS (Master of Science in Information Systems) program welcomed a special guest, Dr. Gao, co-director of CDHAI (https://carey.jhu.edu/cdhai) and a renowned expert in artificial intelligence (AI), to its orientation event. Dr. Gaos visit came as part of the program's ongoing efforts to prepare its students for the rapidly evolving world of technology and business.

During his keynote address, Dr. Gao focused on the transformative impact of AI on various industries. "Artificial intelligence is no longer just a tool; it is reshaping the way businesses operate, enhancing efficiency, and driving innovation," he said. "Every company today, whether a startup or a global enterprise, is looking at how to integrate AI into their business models." Dr. Gao provided real-world examples of how AI is revolutionizing areas such as healthcare, emphasizing the growing need for professionals who not only understand AI but can apply it effectively in business contexts.

When asked why students should choose the MSIS program, Dr. Gao spoke passionately about the curriculum's focus on both technical skills and business acumen. "The MSIS degree offers a unique blend of IT expertise and business strategy, preparing graduates to bridge the gap between technology and organizational goals," he explained. "As AI continues to grow, MSIS graduates will be well-positioned to lead AI-driven transformations in their organizations."

Dr. Gao also highlighted the importance of keeping up with emerging technologies. He praised the MSIS program for continuously evolving to meet market demands, including its new courses on AI essentials, responsible AI, generative AI, human-AI teaming, and machine learning. "This program is not only equipping students with the skills they need today but also preparing them for the future," he concluded.

Dr. Gaos speech left a strong impression on students and faculty, reinforcing MSISs commitment to staying at the forefront of technological innovation. With its forward-thinking approach and close ties to industry leaders, the MSIS program continues to stand out as a top choice for those looking to excel in the intersection of technology and business.
